What is U-Tech?

The fast evolving world of technology where everyone is competing to rise above the others and create the best possible lives with introduction of new technologies and alleviating lifestyles, U-Tech aims to enhance the creative and innovative minds of youngsters and produce the best developers. It provides digital learning for kids whereby, along with academic learning kids have access to new and modern means of developing skills effectively.
That is why U-Tech came into being. We are bunch of undergraduates students who, along with persuing their careers, want to impart their knowledge and expertise on youngsters of our era. We are going to train your kids to the best of our knowledge and we deem it our duty to do so. Our aim is to produce experts in the field of Computer Science that is to become the future of Pakistan.
